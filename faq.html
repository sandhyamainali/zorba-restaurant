Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can we get to Zorba Restaurant?

Zorba the Buddha Restaurant is located in the middle of The Nagarjun Hills. It dwells 5 km from Balaju and 5.5 km from Maccha Pokhari (Ringroad). This heavenly place is 15 min ride from Balaju towards Mudhkhu, Kakani.

2. How can I book a reservation?

To book a reservation, you can go to our Facebook page and direct message us. If you're planning for something special and make it systematic we'll help you. Feel free to reach us via our Instagram page, and Facebook Page.

3. How crowded is Zorba Restaurant?

Zorba the Buddha Restaurant is not much congested. Our customers can enjoy freely as there are many tables. You can enjoy the serenity of nature while enjoying your warm cup of coffee.

4. Does Zorba Restaurant serve vegan meals only?

No, but we do have a vegan menu. We're a vegetarian restaurant and cafe. We use the products of cow's milk so we're not entirely a vegan restaurant. But we do have mouth watery vegan meals.

5. What is the price range of foods and services at Zorba Restaurant?

Every food in Zorba the Buddha Restaurant is quite affordable. It ranges from ($1 - $6). The price of food is quite low, despite high-quality foods and services. Even the expensive food that is served in Zorba the Buddha Restaurant is less than $6. The quality dishes that are to die for, is quite affordable without any adulteration. So it's the perfect spot where you can have fun fully without thinking much of financial trauma.

Unfortunately, NO. Because, we want our customers to value the ideal location where we're dwelled in. So if you're looking for the best vegetarian dishes, then we highly encourage you to come to our destination and appreciate the vicinity of Zorba the Buddha Restaurant. Osho Tapoban Meditation Trip for a Day

Osho, coined as "a most revolutionary man after Jesus Christ "is a visionary, mystic, spiritual leader who conveys the science of healing the inner self via different refined meditation techniques. The restaurant is named after the book written by Osho himself called "Zorba the Buddha" which claims that the ancient Greek god, Zorba is no different than Buddha and the west is not different from the east. Zorba the Buddha restaurant gives an awesome opportunity to explore Osho Tapoban for a day where you can learn the valuable teachings of Osho Rajneesh.
