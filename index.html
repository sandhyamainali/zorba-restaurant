Good Food, Great Place

It's a beautiful location a few miles out of Balaju off a newly paved road. It's peaceful, quiet and the restaurant has pleasant servers. The restaurant is a little pricey compared to most restaurants with a limited vegetarian menu. The pizza is more expensive than the best pizza places in Kathmandu, and the portion is shockingly small for the price. They could do better to offer more veggie sandwiches, to name one item. Espresso coffees are available. No alcohol.

— Yubraj
